Title

Graphics / Web / Branding

Kelly Regencia is currently a freelance Web UI Designer with satisfied clients in Australia and it has given her even more love for this work.  She's always been a creative person. She's interested in finding creative solutions as she believe good design can positively impact the web design industry. Her areas of expertise include Web User Interface and Graphic design with a good understanding of Web UI accessibility.

Although lack of time is always an issue, She's constantly catching up on the latest web design and user interface techniques. One of the reasons that she love web design so much is that it is an ever-evolving medium. It is an amazing sense of accomplishment every time you learn a new technique – especially if it was a struggle to get to that point.

Since taking the “Freelance Plunge”, Kelly is now living the dream of being a small business / web design entrepreneur. She is absolutely enjoying her career, the challenges and the rewards that come with it.
